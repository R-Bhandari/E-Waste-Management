Reverse Logistics

Hinderence in Extended Producer Responsibility Guidelines

One of the biggest factors that are preventing Producers from meeting their Extended Producer Responsibility Guidelines (EPR) and facilitate the recycling of their product, is the logistical cost of collection, transportation, and thereafter its reuse and responsible disposal. This cost is exorbitant, due to the varying geographical terrain of our country.

What we do to Overcome the Problem

To facilitate producers and address this issue, we have established a widespread presence across India to deliver a customized Reverse Logistics service which collects e-waste from the customer’s doorstep and transports it directly to our facility. We ensure this collection within 96 hours of scheduling the pick-up and ensure that both hazardous and non-hazardous e-waste is transported safely, securely and responsibly, through our channels.

Our Advanced Mechanism

We have established an advanced Reverse Logistics mechanism to bring all our customer’s e-waste to our factory in secured transportation with the help of our strong local network, wide presence and channel partners. We deploy our own fleet of vehicles to prevent any inefficiencies and ensure the safe and sustainable disposal of e-waste.

24*7 Support and Logistics Partner

Our Reverse Logistics process provides pan-India services for which we have established partnerships with companies such as FedEx and Gati to serve our customers. To further ensure an easy experience, we provide our customers with 24*7 call center facilities to answer queries relating to their pick-up. Our priority is to facilitate our clients seamlessly and conveniently through the pick-up of e-waste, the client’s on-site operations, and the final reporting.
